About

Hi! 👋🏽 I’m Harish Kumar Sadasivam, a graduate with a Master of Science in Health Informatics from Indiana University Indianapolis and a Doctor of Pharmacy (Pharm.D) from Rajiv Gandhi University of Health Sciences. My journey bridges clinical experience and a strong foundation in data science — with a mission to improve healthcare through technology.

🧠 My Focus #

I specialize in applying data analytics, machine learning, and EMR system design to real-world healthcare problems. My work blends medical knowledge with data engineering to support:

  • Predictive modeling for patient outcomes
  • AI-driven decision support in EMRs
  • Health data standardization using ICD-11, FHIR, HL7
  • Clean, ethical, and interpretable AI

💻 Tools I Use #

  • Languages: Python, SQL, MySQL
  • Analytics & Visualization: Power BI, pandas, seaborn
  • Web Development: React.js, Node.js
  • Databases & Standards: ICD-11, HL7, FHIR

🧭 Why This Matters #

I believe technology should serve patient care — not replace it. I’m driven by the idea that data can make healthcare smarter, safer, and more inclusive. Whether through building user-friendly EMR systems or analyzing trends in chronic disease data, I’m committed to evidence-based, human-centered design.
